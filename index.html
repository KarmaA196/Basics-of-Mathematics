Addition Subtraction
Addition: Adding means putting things together to find out how many you have in total. For example, if you have 2 apples and get 3 more, you now have 5 apples. Subtraction: Subtraction means taking something away to find out what’s left. For example, if you have 5 apples and eat 2, you’ll have 3 apples left.

Multiplication Division
Multiplication: Multiplication is like adding the same number over and over. For example, if you have 3 bags with 4 apples each, you multiply 3 × 4 to get 12 apples in total. Division: Division is splitting something into equal parts or groups. For example, if you have 12 apples and want to share them with 4 friends, each friend gets 3 apples.
