What is Division?

Division is the process of splitting a number into equal parts. It is the inverse operation of multiplication. When you divide, you are distributing a number into smaller, equal groups.

How to Solve One-Digit Division
To divide one-digit numbers, simply divide the dividend by the divisor to find the quotient.
Example: 8 ÷ 4
Step 1: Divide 8 by 4.
Answer: 8 ÷ 4 = 2

In this example, we are starting with 8 apples and dividing them into 4 equal groups.

When we do this, we get 2 apples in each group.

How to Solve Two-Digit Division
To divide two-digit numbers, divide the dividend by the divisor. If the dividend is larger, use long division.Long Division Video
Example: 54 ÷ 9
Step 1: Divide 54 by 9.
Step 2: 54 ÷ 9 = 6.
Answer: 54 ÷ 9 = 6

How to Solve Three-Digit by One-Digit Division
For three-digit by one-digit division, divide the dividend by the divisor, using long division if needed.Long Division Video
Example: 120 ÷ 5
Step 1: Divide 120 by 5.
Step 2: 120 ÷ 5 = 24.
Answer: 120 ÷ 5 = 24

Solve a Word Problem
Sally has 320 apples, and she wants to pack them into boxes with 8 apples each. How many boxes does she need?
Divide the total apples by the number of apples per box.
Try Solving This Problem!

The answer is: 40
